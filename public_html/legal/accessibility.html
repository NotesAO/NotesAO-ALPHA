Accessibility Statement

NotesAO is committed to making our website and platform accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We strive to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1) Level AA.

Measures We Take

  • Keyboard-navigable menus, forms, and interactive elements.
  • Text alternatives (alt attributes) for all meaningful images and icons.
  • Color-contrast ratios of at least 4.5:1 for text and essential graphics.
  • Consistent heading hierarchy and ARIA landmarks for screen readers.
  • Regular automated (axe, Lighthouse) and manual audits by trained staff.

Known Limitations

Some historical PDF exports may not contain full tagging for screen readers. Upon request we will supply an accessible version within five business days.
